Naan Bread

Grocery list





Recipe

  1. put all ingredients together in a bowl big enough.
  2. mix it all together into a dough.
  3. form the dough into a ball, then let it knead.
  4. divide the dough into small balls and roll everyone of them into naan bread shapes.
  5. fry them in a hot dry frying pan until they are golden looking.
